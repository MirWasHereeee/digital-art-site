Why Digital Art?

Digital art is artwork created using digital technology such as tablets, styluses, and creative software. It allows artists to experiment freely and refine their work in ways traditional media cannot.

Many creators love digital art because it is flexible, efficient, and constantly evolving. With the right tools, anyone can begin creating.
