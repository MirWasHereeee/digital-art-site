Popular Digital Art Software

• Procreate – A powerful and beginner-friendly iPad drawing app.

• Clip Studio Paint – Widely used for comics, manga, and illustration.

• Adobe Photoshop – Industry-standard software for digital illustration and design.

• Krita – Free, powerful, and very popular among indie and beginner artists.

• GIMP – Good free alternative for photo editing and basic illustration work.

• Corel Painter – Professional painting software with realistic brush simulation.

• Autodesk SketchBook – Simple, beginner-friendly sketching program.

Digital Art Tools

Most digital artists use a graphic tablet or tablet device paired with professional software to simulate real brushes and pens digitally.
