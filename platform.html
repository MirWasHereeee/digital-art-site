Where to Share Your Digital Art

Mainstream Social Media Platforms:

• Instagram – Great for visual portfolios and short reels.
• TikTok – Popular for speedpaints and art process videos.
• Twitter (X) – Widely used by artists for community engagement.
• Bluesky – Growing platform with a strong art community.
• Tumblr – Good for sharing art, sketches, and fandom-style creative work.
• Pinterest – Excellent for inspiration boards and saving visual portfolio pieces.
• YouTube – Ideal for posting speedpaints, tutorials, and art process videos..

Dedicated Art Communities:

• DeviantArt – Long-standing digital art community.
• ArtStation – Professional portfolio platform for concept artists and illustrators.
• Behance – Creative showcase platform by Adobe.
• Pixiv – Popular among anime and manga artists.
• Newgrounds – Creative community for artists and animators.
